About Rep Tracker

Stay informed! At Rep Tracker we aim for a stronger democracy by supporting voter education at the local and national level. Come election time, significant issues are on the ballot along with names voters need to know. Rep Tracker allows you to search for local or national representatives and provide you with information regarding their political decisions, social media activity, opinions on current issues and a way to get in touch in touch him or her.

Follow your local or state representatives to stay informed about decisions that are important to you and your family. From education, the environment and community developement, Rep Tracker allows to stay on top of how your representatives are voicing your concerns. ad

SUPPORTING VOTER EDUCATION

Stay informed! At Rep Tracker we aim for a stronger democracy by supporting voter education at the local and national level. Come election time, significant issues are on the ballot along with names voters need to know. Rep Tracker allows you to search for local or national representatives and provide you with information regarding their political decisions, social media activity, opinions on current issues and a way to get in touch in touch him/her.
